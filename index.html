Experience

a snapshot of my professional journey

My professional journey has been diverse and exciting. I began my career as a translator for English and Spanish, skills that have been the cornerstone of my professional growth.

For the greater part of my 12 years in Spain, I worked for BASF. First as a team's assistant during a critical SAP implementation project, translating documentation and monitoring the team's progress. Later, in the IT department, where I handled administrative responsibilities and offered second-level support for MS Word, Excel, and PowerPoint. This role highlighted my ability to adapt to technical challenges.

When I returned to Germany, my career continued in various sectors including the Canadian Government and several companies. I worked in Purchasing, Sales, and Administration, which temporarily shifted my focus away from IT. However, my innate strengths remained evident - structured problem-solving, meticulous organization, and a customer-oriented approach that left a positive impression on those I interacted with.

Driven by a profound passion for technology, I decided to change careers in order to do what I love. Eversince, I have been studying front-end development. Check out my certificates to see what I have been up to.
